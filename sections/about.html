About

Building AI that ships to the real world

I'm a Computer Vision & AI Research Engineer currently at Geotab (acquired Smarter AI) in Dubai, where I build edge AI perception systems deployed on fleet dashcams.

My background spans the full stack: designing deep learning architectures in PyTorch, shipping production inference in C++ on Nvidia / Qualcomm SoCs, and building MLOps & CI/CD pipelines that cut field testing time.

Before Geotab, I was an AI Research Engineer at Neubility (making autonomous delivery robots) in Seoul and a Graduate Researcher at KAIST's Vehicular Intelligence Lab in Daejeon, where I published state-of-the-art results in camera pose estimation at ECCV and IEEE ITS.

I'm also pursuing a Global MBA at University of Western Australia to bridge deep technical work with business strategy and product leadership.
